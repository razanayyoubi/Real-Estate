LebEstates Legal & Disclosures

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At LebEstates, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. We collect information through several channels:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and preferences provided during registration, consultation scheduling, or when requesting valuations and legal services.
  • Property Details: Any property data, locations, pricing, and photos you upload when proposing assets for sale or rent.
  • Usage Logs: Information regarding how you navigate our platform, searches performed, and favorited properties to continuously improve UI layout and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized strictly for premium real estate service delivery, including:

  • Providing real-time matches for purchase, sale, or rental inquiries.
  • Fulfilling legal audits, comparative market analyses, and scheduling valuation visits.
  • Maintaining account security, including secure multi-factor authentication (2FA) logs.
  • Improving system interfaces and validating transactions.

3. Information Protection

LebEstates employs state-of-the-art administrative, technical, and physical security measures to shield your records. Personal information and transactional data are stored behind encrypted protocols and accessible only by authorized employees under strict authentication checks.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We share details only with verified legal associates or internal appraisal specialists explicitly chosen by you to handle legal or valuation services, or as required by Lebanese national regulations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, edit, or request the deletion of your registered accounts and associated logs. For any questions regarding data removal or privacy policy inquiries, please contact our support desk at privacy@lebestates.com.
